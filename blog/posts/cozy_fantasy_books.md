## Series to Settle Into
- **Wayward Children (Seanan McGuire):** Portal fantasy novellas with bittersweet returns and supportive dorm-room camaraderie.
- **The Goblin Emperor universe (Katherine Addison):** Court intrigue softened by empathy; etiquette as weaponized kindness.
- **Legends &amp; Lattes + Bookshops &amp; Bonedust (Travis Baldree):** Coffee, book dust, and cozy competence in two flavors.
