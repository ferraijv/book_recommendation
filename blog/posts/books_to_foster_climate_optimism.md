### How to use this list
- **Start where you have energy.** Pick a book that matches your mood—practical, poetic, or page-turning fiction.
- **Read with others.** Form a mini reading circle and assign a different "What you'll do next" action to each person.
- **Apply one idea immediately.** Whether it's electrifying a habit, donating to a frontline group, or sharing a hopeful story, momentum matters.

Climate optimism isn’t wishful thinking—it’s fuel for sustained action. These books highlight where solutions already work and how each of us can help them spread.
